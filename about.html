Our Mission

JET Wrestling Club promotes amateur wrestling in Oconee County by providing young athletes opportunities to learn fundamentals, compete safely, and build leadership.

Coaching & Staff

Coaches are local former wrestlers and certified instructors; many contribute to youth development across the county. JET often partners with local schools and community organizations to host clinics and tournaments.

Programs

  • Summer Camps (skills, conditioning, live wrestling)
  • Weekly practices & open mat sessions
  • Tournaments and the annual JET Open
  • Private lessons and small group clinics

Club Listings & Profiles

JET appears in regional club directories and event listings (TrackWrestling, USAW events, Flowrestling). See sources for official pages.
