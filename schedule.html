Typical Camp Format

Camps run as day camps over multiple consecutive days. A typical day includes warm-up & agility, skill stations, live wrestling, and conditioning.

Example Daily Schedule

  • 8:30 – 9:00 – Check-in & dynamic warm-up
  • 9:00 – 10:30 – Skill stations (takedowns, defense, mat technique)
  • 10:30 – 11:00 – Break / hydration
  • 11:00 – 12:30 – Live wrestling & situational drilling
  • 12:30 – 1:30 – Lunch (participants bring packed lunch)
  • 1:30 – 3:00 – Strength & conditioning / team games

Pricing (example)

Standard registration: $120 per session. Early bird and sibling discounts often available. Tournament coaching and private lessons priced separately.

Exact dates, session lengths, and pricing are announced on JET's official pages and event listings.
