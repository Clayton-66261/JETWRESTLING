Why JET Camp?

JET Wrestling Club is a community-focused wrestling organization based in Seneca and Oconee County, SC. Coaches emphasize technique, safety, and competitive preparedness.

  • Technique stations and live wrestling
  • Strength & conditioning tailored for wrestlers
  • Small group instruction and situational drilling
  • Sportsmanship, discipline, and leadership

Many of JET's events — including the JET Open and seasonal Boot Camps — are hosted at West‑Oak High School's wrestling facilities.
