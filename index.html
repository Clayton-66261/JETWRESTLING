About the Camp

The JET Wrestling Summer Camp is a skill-building camp run by JET Wrestling Club (a youth/recreational wrestling program in Oconee County, SC). The camp focuses on fundamentals, live wrestling, conditioning, and sportsmanship with separate tracks for beginners through advanced wrestlers.

Where & When

Location: West-Oak High School — Wrestling room / Mini Gym (130 Warrior Lane, Westminster, SC).

Typical schedule: multi-day camp (mornings and afternoons), plus specialized technique sessions. Exact dates and session times are announced by JET each season.

Who should attend

  • Kids & teens (elementary → high school) interested in learning or improving wrestling skills.
  • New wrestlers who need fundamentals and experienced wrestlers wanting competitive prep.

What to bring

  • Comfortable athletic wear, wrestling shoes (if available), water bottle, towel.
  • Parents—please bring a waiver form and emergency contact information.
